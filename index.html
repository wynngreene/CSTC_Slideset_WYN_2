Quality Policy

Keysight Technologies Inc. will earn customer loyalty by providing products, solutions, services and interactions of the highest quality and greatest value. To achieve this result, we will:

  • Ensure that all of our products and solutions comply with applicable safety and regulatory requirements.
  • Ensure our products and solutions meet or exceed their published specifications.
  • Maintain and continually improve the effectiveness of our business management systems to conform at a minimum to ISO9001 Quality Management Standard or more stringent or legally required standards as dictated by specific markets.
  • Continually monitor and improve customers’ total experience.
  • Establish quality requirements for suppliers, partners, and contractors and hold them accountable to comply.
  • Treat customers in accordance with Keysight’s Standards of Business Conduct and Privacy policies.
